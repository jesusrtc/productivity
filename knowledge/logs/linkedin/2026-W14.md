### New Feature: `%%safeql` Cell Magic

Built `%%safeql` IPython magic as a drop-in `%%sql` replacement with automatic retry:

```
%%safeql df <<
SELECT * FROM t WHERE datepartition >= daysago({DAYSAGO})
```

- Defaults: retries=3, backoff=5min, exponential backoff, raises after exhausting
- Resolves `{var}` from notebook namespace, unknown braces pass through
- Accepts `<<` for `%%sql` compatibility
- 16 unit tests, E2E validated on Darwin
